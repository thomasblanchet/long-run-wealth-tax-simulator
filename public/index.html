Long-run Wealth Tax Simulator

How much money can you raise with a wealth tax? And how would it affect inequality? Use this simulator answer these questions. Unlike other simulators, it also takes into account how taxation dynamically affect wealth accumulation, so it can estimate its effects in the long run.

The anaysis is based on the paper “Uncovering the Dynamics of the Wealth Distribution”, which develops simple formulas for understaing the long-run impact of wealth taxation.
